Transcript Summary in Email — Get a Copy of Your Conversation

During a chat with Ask Microsoft, customers can request a copy of their conversation transcript via email. The customer enters their details, and the transcript is emailed when the session ends. Chat continues normally after the request.

Transcript Email

How it works

  • Natural conversation: Customer chats with Ask Microsoft about Microsoft 365 plans, features, pricing, or any product question.
  • Transcript offer: At a point during the conversation, the customer is presented with an option to get a copy of the transcript via email.
  • Collection card: If the customer is interested, a card appears asking for their first name and email address, along with a notice about how their information will be used.
  • Confirmation & delivery: Customer submits, gets a confirmation, and the full conversation transcript is emailed to them when the session ends.
  • Chat continues: The conversation keeps going normally after the transcript request — this is not a conversation-ending action.
Key concept: This is a customer-initiated, transactional action. The customer chooses to receive a transcript and provides their details to make that happen. The chat is never interrupted or gated.
Transcript delivery: The full conversation transcript (as-is) is emailed when the session ends — either when the customer wraps up or after an idle timeout.
